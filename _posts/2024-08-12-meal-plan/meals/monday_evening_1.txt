Salmon&Cod with potatoes and tomatoes

Preheat Oven: Heat to 200°C (400°F).
Assemble Dish: Place diced potato, one salmon fillet, one white fish fillet, cherry tomatoes, sliced red onion, minced garlic, and optional zucchini in a baking dish.
Season: Drizzle with olive oil, squeeze lemon juice, sprinkle lemon zest, oregano, smoked paprika, salt, and pepper. Toss gently.
Bake: Cover with foil and bake for 25–30 minutes until fish flakes and potatoes are soft.
Mash Potatoes: Remove fish and vegetables, mash potatoes in the same dish with butter, milk, salt, and pepper. Serve together.
Enjoy!
